RAF Aircraft Activity

Your task is to create a presentation, poster, advert, pamphlet or any other unique presentation on any RAF aircraft of your choice. You don't have to present your project to the squadron if you don't want to, CI Brooks can present it on your behalf if needed. There is no set due date for this currently, the date will be given after the final presentation is delivered on this topic
